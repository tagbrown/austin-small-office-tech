About Us

We started ASOT because small offices deserve better IT support

Not enterprise-grade complexity. Not break/fix techs who vanish. Just reliable, ongoing support from people who actually pick up the phone.

Our story

We've spent years working in IT, and we kept seeing the same problem: small offices were either paying enterprise prices for services they didn't need, or they were stuck with unreliable break/fix techs who treated them as an afterthought.

Meanwhile, someone on their team (usually the youngest or "most tech-savvy" person) became the unofficial IT department. That's not fair to them, and it's not good for the business.

We built Austin Small Office Tech to be the third option: professional, reliable IT support that's actually designed for offices your size. No overkill. No disappearing acts. Just good support from people who know your name.

What drives us

Small offices matter

You're the backbone of Austin's economy. You deserve IT support that treats you that way.

Technology should help, not frustrate

When your tech works, you can focus on what you're actually good at.

Relationships beat transactions

We'd rather have 50 clients who love us than 500 who barely know our name.

Why we're different

We're not trying to be everything to everyone. We're trying to be exactly what small Austin offices need.

Local to Austin

We live here, work here, and can be at your office when you need us. No outsourced call centers, no anonymous tickets.

Right-sized for you

We focus exclusively on offices with 1-15 people. That means we understand your challenges and don't try to sell you enterprise solutions.

Clear Communication

We explain things in ways that make sense. No jargon, no condescension, no making you feel dumb for asking questions.

Proud to serve Austin

We support small offices throughout the Austin metro area, from downtown to the suburbs. In-office, remote, or hybrid, we've got you covered.
