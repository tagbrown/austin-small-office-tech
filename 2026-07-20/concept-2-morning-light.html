03 | The Middle Path

The middle path

"We keep the promise small enough to keep: we answer the phone, we show up, and we stay."

Every small office we meet has tried one of three things. Some hired a big managed-services firm and got a ticket queue, a 40-page agreement, and a bill sized for a company ten times theirs. Some called a break/fix tech who solved one problem and vanished. And most handed the whole mess to whichever staff member seemed good with computers. The accidental IT person, resetting passwords between the tasks they were hired to do.

We built ASOT to be the middle ground: a local Austin team on a month-to-month plan, one number to call, and people who know your setup because they set it up. We'd rather have 50 clients who love us than 500 who barely know our name.

No overkill. No disappearing acts. No drafting your office manager into a second job.
